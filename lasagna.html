Lasagna

Descrption

This classic lasagna recipe is hearty and delicious. Layers of pasta, cheese, and a rich meat sauce make it a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce by cooking ground beef with garlic and onion. Add tomato sauce and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Layer the noodles, ricotta cheese, meat sauce, and shredded mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat the layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden.
